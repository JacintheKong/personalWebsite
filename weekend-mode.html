Me on Weekends :)

My hobby is hiking. I love exploring moderate to difficult trails, and I often spend my weekends out in nature. I've challenged myself with some tough hikes, like the hard trail up Mount Monadnock in New Hampshire, as well as the Beehive, Gorham Mountain, and Ocean Path Loop trails in Acadia National Park.

When I first hiked Mount Monadnock several years ago, I felt an immediate connection with the rugged beauty of the trail. I remember thinking, “This is where I belong.” The steep inclines and rocky paths were challenging, but the views from the summit made every step worth it. Since then, I've been saving up for better hiking gear to tackle even more difficult trails.

I live in Boston, and I often drive out to nearby trails to get my hiking fix. It's a great way to recharge after a busy week, with the journey typically taking about an hour depending on the trail.

The more challenging the trail, the more I feel alive as I navigate each obstacle. Although it's tough at times, the sense of achievement and the breathtaking views at the summit make it worthwhile.

The best season for hiking is fall. The crisp air, the rustling leaves underfoot, and the vibrant colors of the foliage create a perfect backdrop for an unforgettable hiking experience.
